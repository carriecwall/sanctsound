Humpback whales in CINMS

Sights & Sounds

Humpback whales, such as this one breaching in CINMS, make a variety of sounds including moans, cries, groans and clicks. Photo: Barbara LaCorte NOAA/CINMS

This humpback whale call produced between 100 to 900 Hz was recorded in CINMS.

Daily Patterns

Humpback whales were heard most often from late morning through early evening at listening station ## in CINMS between mid-December and mid-April. Humpback whale calls were especially numerous from 10AM-1PM and again from 8-10 PM. Conversely, very few humpback whale calls were heard late at night (11 PM-4 AM).

Monthly Patterns

Humpback whales were heard throughout the study period at listening station ## in CINMS. Humpback whale calls were detected most often in summer months and least often in winter months.
